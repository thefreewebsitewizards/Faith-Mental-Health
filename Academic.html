Academic Requirements & Disclaimers

Final Essay Requirement

To qualify for the Advanced Certificate, students must submit a 1,500-word integration essay demonstrating the synthesis of clinical mental health principles with faith-informed pastoral care. This is a mandatory component for the 10-hour credit certification.

Legal Disclaimer

The courses offered by Faith Informed Mental Health School are for educational and spiritual enrichment purposes only. They do not constitute professional clinical licensure. Students are encouraged to consult local state regulations regarding mental health practice and pastoral counseling boundaries.
